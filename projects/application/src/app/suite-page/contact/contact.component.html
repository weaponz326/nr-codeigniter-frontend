Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

Is my data well protected?

netRink employs SSL and application security. Also, all data are being securely stored on trusted servers with technology-leading harware firewall.

What are netRink's policies regarding our data and data portability?

All rights to your data are maintained by you. We provide you the ability to easily export your data and take it elsewhere, if desired. So long as your account is active, you have full access to your information, for viewing or transfer, at any time.

What type of support is included with your products?

netRink provides flexible support, mainly through our email support service during standard business hours – Monday through Friday, 9AM–5PM GMT.

What are the possible payment methods?

You can select the payment method from the list in the "Payment method" field. Among them you can find credit/debit card, wire (bank) transfer, Yandex.Money, mobile payment, purchase order, cash.

How does netRink leverage the latest technologies?

We ensure that our customers are provided with state of the art technologies with frequent enhancement cycles, with new features rolled out several times each year.

What contract options are available?

The service is made available to you on a monthly or an annual basis through an effective subscription mechanism. This avoids large upfront costs associated with on-premise installation.
